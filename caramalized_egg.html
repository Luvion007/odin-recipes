Caramalized Egg


Description

Caramelized eggs offer a unique twist on traditional egg dishes by adding a rich, sweet flavor through a caramelization process. This technique involves gently cooking the eggs until the sugars caramelize, creating a golden-brown exterior with a creamy, slightly sweet interior. Perfect as a gourmet breakfast or a sophisticated dessert, caramelized eggs combine savory and sweet elements in a delightful way.

Ingredient

  • egg
  • pork
  • brown sugar

Step by Step instructions

  1. boil the brown sugar with water
  2. put the egg and pork in it
  3. let it sit
